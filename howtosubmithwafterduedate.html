How To Submit Homework After Due-Date? | Edunext Technology


Have you crossed the due-date to submit the homework? Donot worry, Today I will guide you how to submit homework after duedate in edunext technology, Just follow the steps.You can also do this if the teacher has checked your homework.


1.Open two window-

open homework in two windows ,in the first one open the homework which has crossed the due-date and in the second window open the homework which has not crossed the date and you can submit your homework there.


2.Open developer tools in both the windows-

open the developer tool and select the submit button


3.Click the three dots-

click the three dots and choose the edit with HTML option .Copy the HTML code


4.Return to the first window which is having the homework which has crossed the duedate-

select the label "You cannot submit homework" and press the three dots, now edit this with HTML and paste the copied code.


5.Add your Homework file-

You can now add your homework files and submit it .But remember do not refresh the website untill you submit the homework .If you do so then the submit button will disappear and you have to do th whole process again.
