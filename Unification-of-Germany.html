Question:
Write a brief note on unification of Germany

Answer:
1.Nationalist feelings were widespread among the middle class Germans, who tried to unite different regions of German confideration into a nation state governed by an elected parliament
2.It was repressed by the combined forces of monarch and military, supported by the landowners called junkers of Prussia. From then Prussia took on the leadership of the movement for national unification.
3.Otto Von Bismarck, the chief minister of Prussia, was the architect of the German Unification. He did this with the help of Prussian army and bureaucracy.
4.Three wars were fought over 7 years with France, Denmark and Austria that ended to Prussian victory and completed the process of unification.
5.In 1871, the Prussian king, William I was proclaimed the German emperor in a ceremony held at Verseilles.
